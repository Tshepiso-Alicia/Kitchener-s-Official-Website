Our Journey

Established in 1906, Kitchener's is housed in a old building that dates back over a century, evolving a simple barely-noticeable pub into a vibrant landmark. Over the years, Kitchener's has transformed into more than a basic bar but now for it's diverse music events, creativity, connectivity, and more. Our place has cultivated a safe space for emerging artists such as musicians, dancers and comedians, as well as everyone else who enters the building seeking our delicious products or rich social activity, our energy is unmatched! Our sibling space, Little Kitchener's, was created from the same essence and love for great food and conversations that cultivate joy, growth and authenticity .

Our current founder, Adam Levy, is a firm believer of the significance of connectivity, what seems like a business of food, drinks and music is actually more than that. He wanted to portray a relaxed and intimate environment for different people while nurturing the creativity and vibrance every indidual possesses within themselves. Kitchener's brings together individuals from different backgrounds or upbringings that have the desire to be a part of the culture and the unique creative movement, there is so much room created for diversity and story-telling, whether we attract an audience looking for an amazing DJ mix that sets the soul on fire, a conversation that ignites new ideas and train of thought, or just a fun time with good friends, old or new, and great drinks. You are not just a part of our story, you are the reason we have a story!

Our Mission Statement and Values

At Kitchener's, our mission is to create a space for creatives, music lovers and a community driven by a diverse culture, we believe in authenticity, connection and creativity that ensures that every individual who enters our premises gets embraced by a sense of belonging and welcoming atmosphere. We are dedicated to preserving the spirit of Johannesburg and it's young, vibrant nightlife that serves as the main reason we so connected as a community, while maintaining innvotive ways to adapt and thrive in the ever evolving world we live in. Whether it is through curating fresh music lineups, hosting unique cultural events or improving our customers' experiences, the goal is remain a in the Braamfontein social experience. Our values include:

  • Quality and esperience: From our carefully made cocktails and brewed beers to our finely curated sound experience, we prioritise excellence in every aspect of our service.
  • Cultural expression: We support artists, musicians, creatives and culturally inclined indviduals by providing a platform for their productions to be recognised and celebrated.
  • Community and inclusivity: We welcome individuals from different backgrounds to support fostering of an environment that is open, diverse and vibrant enough to create beatiful experiences and innovation.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable business practices, avoiding a creation of waste and ensuring that we source locally produced ingridients and services in order to uplift the ecosystem we reside in.
