Kitchener's Bar Menu

At Kitchener's, we prioritise producing fresh drinks that reveal our love of quality, while fostering an equally enjoyable environment to dine in. Our drinks are produced from fresh fruits quality alcohol, we take pride in it. We offer a variety of alcoholic and non-alocoholic beverages, from innovative cocktails and mocktails to classic wines and crafted beers, our bartenders create a wide range of drinks for every customer.

We offer something for everyone.

Best-selling Cocktail

Orange Blossom: Our bartenders create this with Amarula gin, orgeat syrup, bitters and grapefruit, including custom additions when possible.

Best-selling Mocktail

Strawberry Lemonade: A light and fizzy lemonade made from strawberry syrup, lemon tonic and mint leaf. Available in a variety of fruity flavors.



Little Kitchener's Pizzeria Menu

At Little Kitchener's, we serve mouth-watering pizza dishes, salads and spritzers, the bold flavours, fresh ingredients and dishes are all pawns used to make a memorable experience. Our signature dishes represent the energy of Kitchener's as a harmoneous establishment, it is authentic, full of personality and always satisfying. We believe that food is a part of the engine fuel that sparks up the vibrant space and conversations.

Eat. Dink. Vibe. Repeat.

Best-selling Pizza

The Spicy Chicken: Alightly charred crispy crust, with tomatoes, peppadews, flor di latte, and peri-peri chicken. A classic South African spicy flavour.

Best-selling Salad

Panzanella Summer Salad: This deliciously juicy salad consists of crotons, cucumber, rosa tomatoes, basil, mozzarella and shallots.

Best-selling Spritzer

Grapefruit:Grapefruit, citrus, cinanzo and soda. These tantalising assorted spritzers are available in a variety of flavors, including lemongrass, mango and jalapeno, and watermelon.
